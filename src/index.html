Explain (short)

Feed is the amount of natural/low-enriched uranium you must supply to make a given amount of enriched product. Tails is what remains after enrichment, with a residual U-235 fraction xt.

Lowering xt extracts more U-235 from the feed (less feed needed) but requires more separation effort (more SWU). The economically optimal xt depends mainly on uranium price vs SWU price.

This is a simplified teaching tool (no cascade details, losses, constraints, contracts, etc.).
